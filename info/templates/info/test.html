Instructions

Create new Migration

  • Start a new migration. You can run a Free Demo Migration before purchasing a Full package.

Migration Detail

  • Click on "Migration ID:XXX" for detailed information of your migration. Here, you can find your migration status, available actions, migration history, migration log and your expense.

Migration Status

  • You can find migration status on the right of "Migration ID: XXXX". This describe the current state of your migration.

Continue Migration

  • Continue the unfinished action.

Recent Migration

  • This action is available after you've completed a Full Migration. Recent Migration allows you to migrate ONLY NEW ENTITIES added to your Source Store during and after you performed the last Full Migration.

Re-Migration

  • This action is available after you have completed a Full Migration. Re-migration allows you to CLEAR MIGRATED DATA AND MIGRATE AGAIN. It also allows you to change your Target Store URL before the re-migration.

Smart Update


This action is available after you've completed a Full Migration. Smart Update allows you to:
  • UPDATE INFORMATION of migrated data on Target Store from Source Store. Fields supported in this update are: product name, SKU, quantity, status, product URL key, product price, sales price, product category link.
  • Perform the same function as Recent Data Migration: migrate NEW ENTITIES added to your Source Store during and after you performed the last Full Migration.
Smart Update is perfect when you need an effective update from your Source Store to your Target Store after you have finished Full Migration.
